Entity Dossier: The Guide

December 2, 2025

Not all threats are tactical. Some can't be engaged. Some can't be evaded. Some are simply... waiting. The following dossier was compiled from firsthand accounts. It is not meant to prepare you for combat. It is meant to prepare you for inevitability.

There are entities in the Network's files that don't fit standard classification. They're not threats in the conventional sense—not something you can shoot, outmaneuver, or escape. They serve a function. They occupy a role. And when your time comes, you will meet them whether you want to or not.

This one waits in the space between. He has a job to do. And he's very, very good at it.

Arbiter Class Eyes Only

Network Entity Dossier

CLASSIFICATION: OPERATOR EYES ONLY

SUBJECT DESIGNATION: The Guide

KNOWN ALIASES: None confirmed

ENTITY CLASS: Arbiter (Unique Designation)

ORIGIN: Abrahamic/Mesopotamian (Probable Ifrit subclass)

OPERATIONAL DOMAIN: Limbo / First Circle / Transitional Space

MATERIAL PLANE ACTIVITY: None confirmed

THREAT POSTURE: Non-hostile (Functional)

COMPILED BY: Oracle // Network Intelligence Division

FIELD ASSET RENDERING // ACCURACY UNVERIFIED

Physical Profile

Humanoid manifestation. Height estimated 6'5" to 6'7" (195-200cm). Muscular build consistent with heavy labor or combat conditioning. Head: completely bald. Facial hair: thin beard terminating in neat goatee. Skin tone variable between observations—pallid gray to Mediterranean bronze noted.

Distinguishing feature: EYES. Proportions structurally incorrect. Iris-to-sclera ratio inconsistent with human norm. Color documented as "brown" but witnesses report sensation of "looking into depth without bottom." Surveillance equipment fails to render eyes accurately—result consistently registers as visual artifact or corruption. Recommend treating optical distortion as identifying characteristic.

Subject observed wearing simple human attire (shirt, pants, boots) described by witness as "costume approximating human dress without understanding why humans wear it." Dermal inscriptions visible on exposed skin—identified as pre-Sumerian cuneiform, Aramaic script, and unclassified glyphs. Inscriptions may be inherent rather than applied. Further analysis pending.

Observed Behavior

Function: Processing and orientation of souls in transitional space. Subject operates as guide and arbiter—conducting "tours" of infernal geography and rendering judgment on the damned. Demeanor documented as casual, professorial, conversational. Does not display overt hostility. Does not need to.

Communication style: Deep voice, resonant, described as "wrong" in unspecified way. Speaks with dark amusement. Takes evident pleasure in function without cruelty—satisfaction of competence rather than sadism. Demonstrates knowledge of subject's complete history, actions, and moral standing. Source of knowledge unknown. Assume comprehensive.

Physical capability: Unknown upper limit. Presence induces involuntary compliance in witnesses—subjects report inability to resist verbal commands despite conscious desire to do so. Whether this represents supernatural compulsion or psychological response to authority remains undetermined.

DOCUMENTED VERBAL PATTERN // SIGNIFICANCE: HIGH

"You neeeeever knooooow."

Known Encounters

ENCOUNTER 001 // OPERATOR: J. CREED // LOCATION: First Circle (Limbo)

Context: Near-death experience following IED detonation, Fallujah, Iraq. Operator Creed experienced extended exposure to subject during involuntary tour of infernal geography. Subject demonstrated comprehensive knowledge of operator's investigation into CLASSIFIED ritual activity. Tour included observation of punishment mechanisms across multiple circles. Operator returned to material plane following divine intervention (MIKE). Psychological impact: Severe. Ongoing.

ENCOUNTER 002 // SUBJECT: [NAME WITHHELD] // LOCATION: Limbo (Processing)

Context: Secondhand account via Operator Creed. Details classified pending operational review. Confirms subject's function extends beyond guided observation to active judgment of souls. Utilized Aramaic phrase consistent with Encounter 001. Judgment criteria: ACTIONS. Circumstances and intentions deemed irrelevant. No appeal process observed.

Operational Assessment

Subject is not a threat in conventional terms. Subject is an inevitability. No engagement protocols exist because engagement is not possible. Subject operates exclusively in transitional/infernal space—no material plane manifestation documented. This may change. Recommend monitoring for crossover indicators.

Implications for operators: Subject's judgment criteria appear absolute. Acts matter. Intentions do not. Circumstances do not. Operators should consider this when making operational decisions. The Network does not dictate morality. The Network provides information. What you do with it determines what happens when you meet him.

Counterintelligence value: Subject demonstrated awareness of classified Network operations during Encounter 001. Source of intelligence unknown. Possible penetration of Network information security or inherent omniscience regarding subjects under observation. Recommend REDACTED protocols until determination made.

— END DOSSIER —

ADDENDUM // ORACLE:

Operators have asked why this dossier exists if engagement is impossible. Valid question. Answer: Because you need to know what's waiting. Because decisions made under fire have consequences that extend beyond the material. Because "I didn't know" has never been accepted as defense in his court. This dossier is not tactical preparation. It is existential orientation. You're welcome. Query regarding prayer efficacy declined—insufficient data, ask Clark.

He's patient. He's thorough. And he remembers everything you've ever done.

Sleep well.
