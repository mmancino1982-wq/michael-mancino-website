Chloe Brennan Dubois: Beauty, Blood Money, and Brutal Consequence

January 8, 2026

Chloe's story is one of the most brutal elements in Broken Creed, but it's also one of the most important. Her death isn't gratuitous - it's the moment that breaks Jonah and remakes him into something harder, colder, and infinitely more dangerous.

DECEASED Eyes Only

Network Entity Dossier

CLASSIFICATION: OPERATOR EYES ONLY

SUBJECT DESIGNATION: Chloe Brennan Dubois

KNOWN ALIASES: None

CLASSIFICATION: Human / Catalyst / Civilian

ORIGIN: Houston, TX

PROFESSION: Gallery Director (Elysian Fields)

OPERATIONAL DOMAIN: Deceased (Formerly Houston/Kansas)

STATUS: EXPIRED (Ritual Sacrifice)

ASSOCIATES: Jonah Creed (Partner), Evelyn Finch (Former Roommate)

COMPILED BY: Network Intelligence Division

SUBJECT ID PHOTO // CIRCA 2024

Background Intelligence

Subject served as Gallery Director at Elysian Fields. Intelligence confirms subject was a former college roommate of Evelyn Finch and acted as an acquisitions facilitator for Finch's private collection. Financial audits reveal subject received between $90,000 and $180,000 in undocumented payments. Subject consistently claimed ignorance regarding the true nature of Finch's operations. Targeted by the Erinyes for collection of "blood money" debt.

Physical & Psychological Profile

Physical: Late 30s. Features include thick, dark hair and bright green eyes. Complexion described as warm olive Mediterranean.

Psychological: Highly intelligent and professionally competent. Demeanor observed as passionate about art, maintaining a poised exterior that masked significant anxious energy. Capable of deep interpersonal connection with high empathy. Status at time of death: Romantically involved with Operator Jonah Creed.

Incident Report: The Abduction

INCIDENT DATE: [REDACTED] // LOCATION: Karnak, Egypt

Subject was abducted from a secure safe house in Kansas by three Erinyes operatives following Operator Creed's confrontation regarding illicit payments. Subject was transported to Karnak, Egypt.

Methodology: Subject underwent ritual torture by cultist faction. Kept conscious via administration of black ichor during organ removal process. Event was livestreamed to Operator Creed (orchestrated by Finch and Fury operative).

Final Disposition: Subject judged in Limbo by The Guide. Declared "weighed, measured, and found wanting" (Mene, mene, tekel, upharsin). Condemned to purgatory.

Operational Impact Assessment

Subject's death represents Operator Creed's most significant operational failure to date. Psychological trauma resulting from witnessing subject's torture and execution has resulted in a complete behavioral shift in Operator Creed ("Ice-Cold" state). This event served as the direct motivation for the unsanctioned execution of Evelyn Finch. Subject's fate serves as the defining emotional crucible for Operator Creed's current psychological profile.
