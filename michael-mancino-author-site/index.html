What happens when corporate espionage becomes cosmic horror?

Jonah Creed is a former Army HUMINT collector who spent years gathering intelligence in Iraq—until an IED blast killed him, gave him a tour of Hell, and brought him back marked by something he can neither see nor escape.

Now working as a private investigator in Houston, Jonah is hired to investigate suspected corporate espionage. What begins as a straightforward case spirals into something far darker—a global conspiracy, dimensional breaches, and the same warped symbol he saw scrawled in blood in a Fallujah ritual chamber twenty years ago.
