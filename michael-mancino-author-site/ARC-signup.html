What is an ARC Reader?

ARC stands for "Advance Review Copy." As an ARC reader, you'll receive a free copy of BROKEN CREED before it officially launches. In exchange, you'll read the book and post an honest review on Amazon (and anywhere else you review books) on launch day.

What You Get:

  • Free advance copy in EPUB format
  • Exclusive behind-the-scenes updates
  • Early access to Book 2 announcements
  • Recognition as an early supporter

What I Ask:

  • Read the book at your own pace
  • Post an honest review on Amazon on launch day (January 1, 2026)
  • Optionally share on Goodreads, social media, or other platforms
  • Keep the ARC copy for personal use only (no sharing or distribution)

About BROKEN CREED:

Genre: Psychological Horror / Military Fiction

Length: 123,000 words

Content Warning: This novel contains graphic violence, torture, body horror, and depictions of genuine evil. Not for the faint of heart.

What to expect: Grounded tactical noir that escalates to cosmic horror. A story about what happens when the darkness you left behind in a war zone follows you home, and the only way forward is straight through Hell itself.
